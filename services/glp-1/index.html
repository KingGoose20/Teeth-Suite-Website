PRE PROCEDURE GLP-1 MANAGEMENT

Are you on a GLP-1 receptor agonist for management of weight loss or Diabetes?


Semaglutide – Ozempic, Wegovy
Tirzepatide – Mounjaro
Liraglutide – Victoza, Saxenda
Dulaglutide – Trulicity

These medications have been associated with cases of retained stomach contents and pulmonary aspiration (fluid/food entering the lungs) during sedation for endoscopic procedures or general anaesthesia.
